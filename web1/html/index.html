What is DuckDuckGo?

DuckDuckGo is a privacy-focused search engine that doesn't save your search history and blocks any cookies that monitor and track your online activities. You're free to browse the web, use social media, do online shopping, and much more in anonymity. The search engine launched on September 25, 2008. The name was inspired by the game Duck, Duck, Goose, to make online privacy simple and accessible for everyone. Over the years, the search engine reached new levels, registering hundreds of millions of search queries daily to supporting multiple platforms and devices.
